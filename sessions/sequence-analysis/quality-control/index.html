Quality Control

Below are video tutorials for this GTN session, created for various (past) events.

In this sesssion, we will walk you through the basics of quality checking your sequencing data. This session consists of a lecture followed by a hands-on tutorial.

Quality Control (Lecture)

Description:

This lecture goes over the concepts involved in assessing the quality of your sequencing data.
Length: 40 minutes
Captions: Yvonne Hiltemann, Nadia Goué, Helena Rasche
Created: 15 February 2021
Materials:

Learning Objectives:
  • Assess short reads FASTQ quality using FASTQE 🧬😎 and FastQC
  • Assess long reads FASTQ quality using Nanoplot and PycoQC
  • Perform quality correction with Cutadapt (short reads)
  • Summarise quality metrics MultiQC
  • Process single-end and paired-end data
