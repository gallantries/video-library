Pre-processing of 10X Single-Cell RNA Datasets

Demo Video

Description:

10x genomics has provided not only a cost-effective high-throughput solution to understanding sample heterogeneity at the individual cell level, but has defined the standards of the field that many downstream analysis packages are now scrambling to accommodate. Please work through the full tutorial yourself to get all the details.
